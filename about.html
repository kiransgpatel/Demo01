    History

    VENTURA INC came into being in January 2019. After working for leading MNCs and Trading Houses for over two decades, the founder embarked upon his vision of making the complex world of Trading and Supply Chain Management simpler, nimbler and more transparent.

    Wha we do

    At Ventura, we take pride in being a small but important part of the Global Supply Chain wherein we connect multiple producers of various products to multiple end consumers.

    Our strength lies in searching & researching the best and optimum source of any product across the Globe, developing models for the best practices to grow, procure, pack, transport and distribute those products in a sustainable, cost-efficient way while managing various risks along the way

    To support our primary business activity of supply chain management, we also have dedicated teams to manage Freight & Foreign Exchange Risks.

    Our Strategy

    Ventura Inc is a leading supply chain management company with a focus in the Agri Commodity space. We believe in managing each leg of the value chain in order to give our clients the best possible benefits that each part has to offer. We go upstream to procure the best quality goods at the lowest possible level of the value chain, manage every leg of the supply chain ourselves and offer only the best quality products to our customers. This helps us maintain consistent quality, visibility and control over the entire value chain in order to ensure 100% transparency for our customers.

    Ethics & Compliance

    At VENTURA, ethical working and compliant business policies are embedded into our DNA from the day we came into being. Integrity is the most prized possession of all people at Ventura and we rate this at the top of the qualitative requirements for anyone who wants to be a part of the Ventura world – be in customers, employees, suppliers and all business associates and partners. We have ZERO tolerance of any kind of unethical behaviour, corruption, bribery and encourage people to raise any such issue by writing to us at info@venturaincorporated.com

    Our Services

    FOREIGN EXCHANGE:

    We manage currency risk for inhouse businesses in order to mitigate the FX risk/volatility for all our clients who trade in different geographies with different currencies.

    Our FX desk thru its deep research helps us manage the FX risk by suitable hedging strategies in the Forwards/Options market dealing with first class banks and market makers. As a strategic principle, we DO NOT take Proprietary positions on the FX market. All our FX trades are hedges for our underlying physical goods contracts.

    FREIGHT

    Our freight desk manages all our exposure on the ocean freight market – both containers and break bulk. We execute our freight contracts with leading Container-carriers and Dry-Bulk operators.
