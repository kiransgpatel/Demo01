    Our Strategy
    Ventura Inc is a leading supply chain management company with a focus in the Agri Commodity space. We believe in managing each leg of the value chain in order to give our clients the best possible benefits that each part has to offer. We go upstream to procure the best quality goods at the lowest possible level of the value chain, manage every leg of the supply chain ourselves and offer only the best quality products to our customers. This helps us maintain consistent quality, visibility and control over the entire value chain in order to ensure 100% transparency for our customers.

    Our Services

    We manage currency risk for inhouse businesses in order to mitigate the FX risk/volatility for all our clients who trade in different geographies with different currencies.

    Our FX desk thru its deep research helps us manage the FX risk by suitable hedging strategies in the Forwards/Options market dealing with first class banks and market makers.

    As a strategic principle, we DO NOT take Proprietary positions on the FX market. All our FX trades are hedges for our underlying physical goods contracts.

What We Do?

At Ventura, we take pride in being a small but important part of the Global Supply Chain wherein we connect multiple producers of various products to multiple end consumers.

Our strength lies in searching & researching the best and optimum source of any product across the Globe, developing models for the best practices to grow, procure, pack, transport and distribute those products in a sustainable, cost-efficient way while managing various risks along the way

To support our primary business activity of supply chain management, we also have dedicated teams to manage Freight & Foreign Exchange Risks.
